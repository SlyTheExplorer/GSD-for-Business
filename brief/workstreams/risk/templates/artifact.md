## 7. Quarterly Review Cadence

Each risk has owner + next-review-date. The Quarterly Business Review (QBR) includes a
risk-register diff against the prior quarter:
- New risks added this quarter
- Risks closed (mitigated to acceptable residual)
- Risks elevated (likelihood or impact increased)
- Risks demoted (likelihood or impact decreased)

The first 3 quarters' review dates are pre-populated; subsequent dates are added at
each QBR.
