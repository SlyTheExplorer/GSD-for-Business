## Sensitivity bands

The 12-month projection (sibling artifact `financial-model.md`) renders 3 scenarios:

- **Bear:** every revenue / customer-side driver × 0.7. **Exception: COSTS multiply by
  1.3** (costs go UP in bear — this inversion makes the scenario semantically
  meaningful; without it, "bear" becomes a math noop).
- **Base:** every driver × 1.0.
- **Bull:** every revenue / customer-side driver × 1.3. **Exception: COSTS multiply by
  0.7** (costs go DOWN in bull — symmetric inversion).
